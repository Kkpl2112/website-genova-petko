Our Story
Sport Max was founded by Max Harper, a former college athlete who decided to turn his love for sports into a business. With a passion for both competitive sports and helping others achieve their personal fitness goals, Max opened the doors to Sport Max over two decades ago. Since then, the store has become a staple in the community, known not only for its wide range of products but also for the personalized service that Max and his team provide.
We’re proud to be part of this community and to support local athletes of all ages and skill levels. Whether you’re just getting started or you’re training for a competition, we’re here to help you succeed.
Come by Sport Max and experience the difference. Whether you need advice, the latest gear, or just want to talk sports, we’re here to help. We look forward to helping you reach your full potential.
Thank you for choosing Sport Max—your partner in sports and fitness!
